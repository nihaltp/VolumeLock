<h3>Volume Lock gives you fine-grained control over your Android audio behavior with two core features</h3>

<ul>
<li>Volume Lock: when the screen turns off, current media, ring, notification, and alarm volumes are locked and automatically restored if changed.</li>
<li>App Volume Lock: saves and restores media volume for selected apps when you return to them.</li>
</li>
</ul>

<h3>Key highlights</h3>
<ul>
<li>Tracks and restores audio levels automatically</li>
<li>Lets you choose which apps participate in App Volume Lock</li>
<li>Keeps behavior consistent after reboot</li>
</li>
</ul>

<h3>Permissions</h3>
<ul>
<li>Do Not Disturb / Notification Policy access is needed to lock ring and notification volumes on Android 6+</li>
<li>Accessibility service is needed to detect foreground app changes for App Volume Lock</li>
</li>
</ul>
